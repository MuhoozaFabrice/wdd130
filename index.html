Uganda

Uganda, the "Pearl of Africa," boasts a rich history and culture, encompassing tales of creation myths, kingdoms, and colonial influences. One prominent story is the Ugandan folktale of Kintu and Nambi, which explains the origin of humanity and the establishment of the Baganda kingdom. The country also experienced British colonial rule, leading to the Uganda Protectorate in 1894, and later gained independence in 1962.
